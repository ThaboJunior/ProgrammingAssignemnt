Instruction on how run the program:

1.Click the start button on the visual studio app
2.The menu will appear,choose 1 of the 6 options
3.option 1 will allow you to create a recipe
4.option 2 will display the recipe you entered(wrote)
5.option 3 allows you to scale the recipe
6.option 4 lets you reset the quantities to original values(before thry were scaled)
7.option 5 lets you clear the recipe if maybe you made a mistake and lets you enter a new one
8.option 6 lets you exit the program
